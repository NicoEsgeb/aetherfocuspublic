Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours.

AetherFocus is built with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This policy explains what the app does and does not do.

Effective date: April 6, 2026

No data collection

AetherFocus does not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, or any personally identifiable information. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking of any kind.

Local storage only

All your preferences, notes, tasks, and settings are stored locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your machine and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Microphone access

AetherFocus may request microphone permission to drive audio-reactive visuals. Microphone input is processed in real time on your device to animate visual elements. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted. You can deny this permission and the app will function normally without reactive visuals.

System audio access

AetherFocus may request Screen Recording permission to read system audio output. This allows visual spheres to react to audio from other applications. No screen content is captured or recorded. The audio stream is processed locally in real time and is not stored or transmitted.

In-app purchases

AetherFocus uses Apple's StoreKit framework to handle premium purchases. All transactions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or transaction details.

No third-party services

AetherFocus does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking frameworks. The app is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks and has zero external dependencies.

Children's privacy

AetherFocus does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected from any user, no special provisions are necessary.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Since we do not collect contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.
