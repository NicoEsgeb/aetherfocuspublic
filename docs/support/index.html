Common questions

Permissions

Why does AetherFocus need microphone access?

The microphone drives the visual spheres — they react to live sound like claps, voice, or nearby music. No audio is recorded or stored. You can deny this permission and the app works fine without it.

Why does it ask for screen recording / system audio?

This lets the spheres react to audio from other apps (Spotify, YouTube, etc.). macOS bundles this under the Screen Recording permission. AetherFocus does not capture or record your screen — it only reads the audio stream.

Common questions

General

Is AetherFocus available on iOS?

Not yet — the current release is macOS only. An iOS version is planned for the future.

How do I restore my purchases?

Premium purchases are tied to your Apple ID and restore automatically. If they don't appear, make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID you used to purchase, then restart the app.

Can I use AetherFocus offline?

Yes. All music, sounds, and visuals are bundled in the app. No internet connection is needed after download.
