Common questions

Permissions

Why does AetherFocus ask for microphone access?

The microphone is used only to drive sphere reactivity from live sound such as claps, voice, and nearby audio input.

Why does AetherFocus ask for system audio or screen capture access?

System audio access lets the visual spheres react to audio from other apps such as Spotify or YouTube.
