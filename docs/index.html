Focus sanctuary for macOS

Your desk becomes a world.

AetherFocus wraps your workspace in handcrafted atmospheres — ambient rain over a medieval tavern, wind through a starlit forest, firelight in a stone library. Layer sound, play music, and watch reactive visuals pulse with the audio around you.

Worlds, not wallpapers

Each atmosphere is a full environment — layered video, ambient sound, and reactive light designed to hold your attention without stealing it. Switch worlds to match your mood or the work ahead.

Sound you can sculpt

Blend curated music with ambient textures like rain, crackling fire, forest wind, and night creatures. Every layer has its own volume — build the exact soundscape your focus needs.

Visuals that listen

Eleven unique visual spheres respond to your microphone or system audio in real time — from drifting nebulae to fractal lattices. They breathe with the room so your workspace feels alive.

Built for deep work

Pomodoro timers, a scratchpad for quick notes, and a task list — all inside the app. No tab-switching, no distractions. Just the tools you need alongside the atmosphere that keeps you locked in.
